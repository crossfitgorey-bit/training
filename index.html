Strength & Skills

Specialist Programs

Choose a program that targets your goal. Each is designed to complement — not replace — your CrossFit Gorey class training.

🏋️

CrossFit · 8 Weeks

Skills Accelerator

Master the gymnastics and barbell skills that separate good athletes from great ones. Muscle-ups, handstands, double-unders, and Olympic technique broken into achievable progressions.

  • Gymnastics progressions (pull-ups → muscle-up)
  • Handstand & HSPU development
  • Double-under skill ladders
  • Olympic lifting technique work
Intermediate
⚡

Weightlifting · 12 Weeks

Olympic Lifting

A structured 3-day programme focused on improving your Snatch and Clean & Jerk. Combines technique sessions with strength accessories over 12 weeks with a full peaking phase.

  • 3 lifting sessions per week (45–60 min)
  • Snatch + C&J technique + heavy singles
  • Pulling and squatting accessories
  • Peaking & PR attempts in Phase 3
Intermediate
🔩

Powerlifting · 12 Weeks

Strength Base

Build a bigger squat, bench, and deadlift with this 3-day powerlifting programme. Uses RPE-based loading to systematically drive up your total over 3 months.

  • Squat, Bench, Deadlift 3× per week
  • Accessory work for weak-point development
  • RPE-based loading (no 1RM testing)
  • Optional competition prep block
All Levels

Endurance

Running Programs

Structured plans from 5K to Half Marathon. Run days slot around your WOD days so volume is managed carefully.

🏃

Running · 8 Weeks

5K Program

Go from inconsistent runner to confident 5K finisher. Builds aerobic base with 3 runs per week including one speed session per fortnight.

  • 2–3 runs per week (20–40 min)
  • Easy base runs + one tempo/week
  • Race-day strategy session in week 7
  • Mobility & recovery protocols included
All Levels
🏃

Running · 10 Weeks

10K Program

Bridge the gap to double digits. Adds a long run, structured intervals, and a race-pace session each week to build speed and endurance simultaneously.

  • 3 runs per week (up to 60 min long run)
  • Interval sessions (800m repeats)
  • Threshold runs for lactate development
  • 10K race-pace tune-up in week 9
Intermediate
🏅

Running · 14 Weeks

Half Marathon

A smart 14-week build for athletes combining endurance training with CrossFit volume. Long runs peak at 19–20km with careful loading to prevent injury.

  • 4 runs per week (1 long, 2 medium, 1 speed)
  • Long runs peak at 19–20km (wk 11–12)
  • Taper weeks 13–14 for race freshness
  • Strength maintenance sessions included
Advanced

Injury Management Programs

Designed by coaches with physiotherapy input. These programs keep you moving and rebuilding safely — while your class training continues wherever possible.

💪

Injury Management · Ongoing

Upper Body Rehab

For shoulder, elbow, and wrist issues. Covers rotator cuff strains, bicep tendinopathy, and wrist impingement. Focuses on restoring movement before loading.

  • Phase 1: Pain-free movement restoration
  • Phase 2: Stability & motor control
  • Phase 3: Strength & return to loading
  • Replaces upper body WOD elements safely
All Levels
🦵

Injury Management · Ongoing

Lower Body Rehab

For knee, hip, ankle, and lower back complaints. Addresses patellar tendinopathy, hip impingement, and plantar fasciitis with progressive load management.

  • Phase 1: Unloaded mobility & activation
  • Phase 2: Single-leg stability work
  • Phase 3: Return to squatting & running
  • Coach check-in prompts every 2 weeks
All Levels
⚠️ Medical disclaimer: These programs are general in nature and are not a substitute for professional physiotherapy or medical advice. Always consult a qualified health professional before beginning a rehabilitation programme. Inform your CrossFit Gorey coach of any injury before class.
